FAQs

Can I work while I am studying in the UK?

Yes you can work up to 20 hours a week during your studies and full-time during your holidays. This is applicable only if you are studying a degree level course at a UK higher education institution for longer than 6 months.

When can I apply for visa?

  • You will need the Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS) from chosen university.
  • Evidence for paid course fee
  • Maintenance fund evidence
  • Qualification document
  • English proficiency certificate
  • Any other supporting documents

    • Can I bring my family to the UK with me?

    Yes you can bring your family (partner and children) to the UK. You cannot bring your family to the UK as a Tier 4 dependant if you are studying an English language course, a course which is below degree level or an undergraduate course.

    How much does the Tier 4 student visa cost?

    Visa fee plus the Immigration Health Service Charge. For more info please contact us.
